About Us, About LOUGOU!

LOUGOU, a well-established footwear distributor and retailer for Men’s, Women’s and Children leading the top branders to provide the best options for you.

LOUGOU began as a single store in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, and has since extended throughout Malaysia and Singapore, with a total of 100 locations by 1996. LOUGOU has dominated the majority of Malaysia by 2018, especially in Kuala Lumpur, Sabah, Sarawak, and Singapore. LOUGOU was already known as the most distinctive visual merchandiser of sportswear with the greatest and most exclusive style variety at the time.

Fast forward to 2022, LOUGOU officially launched its first virtual sportswear store in Asia, right here in Malaysia. In November, LOUGOU opened its first flagship store in Pavilion Elite, Kuala Lumpur, and its second store in Asia in Sunway Pyramid, Subang Jaya.
